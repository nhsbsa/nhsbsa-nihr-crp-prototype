About the study

The 'Glucose Perception in Type 2 Diabetes (Phase 1)' study aims to compare the taste sensitivity and liking between people living with diabetes and those without diabetes.

The study team is looking for a total of 140 participants who have been diagnosed with type 2 diabetes.

Who can take part

You may be able to take part if you fulfil all of the following criteria:

  • You have been diagnosed with type 2 diabetes.
  • You have received this diagnosis in the last 6 months
  • You are currently take Metformin.
