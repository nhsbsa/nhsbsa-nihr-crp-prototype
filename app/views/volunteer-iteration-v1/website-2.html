What you will do

At your first lab visit, you will complete short questionnaires about stress, anxiety, and fear of pain. Researchers will then test your pain sensitivity (by applying gentle pressure to your neck, arm, and leg) and measure your neck movement and muscle activity during simple tasks (e.g. pushing against a handheld device or holding a neck position).

Over the next 12 months, you will complete a short online questionnaire every two weeks about any new episodes of neck pain and factors such as physical activity, work, mood, and sleep.

If you experience a new episode of neck pain during this time, researchers may invite you for an optional second 2-hour lab session, repeating the same measures as at baseline.

Incentives and reimbursement

  • £20 Amazon voucher for each lab visit
  • £10 Amazon voucher for every two follow-up questionnaires completed

Number of visits

In person lab assessment and 12 months of online follow-up.

Study duration

12 months

Other details

This study is being run with the support of the National Institute for Health and Care Research, working in partnership with the University of Birmingham and University of Essex, and founded by the Medical Research Council.
