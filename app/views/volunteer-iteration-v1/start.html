We need to check that you:

How we handle your data

We'll only use your data to check if you're eligible to take part in this study. If you are not eligible to take part in this study, we'll delete your data immediately.

Your data will only be stored if you successfully pass the pre-screening questions. If you are unsuccessful, your data will be instantly deleted. If you are successful, your data will only be kept for as long as it is needed for the research study.
