Creating a pre-screener helps you match eligible volunteers to your study.

You can use the pre-screener to ask questions about:

  • health conditions
  • medications
  • any other eligibility requirements

When volunteers complete the pre-screener, we’ll collect their personal data, including their age.

Before you start

You’ll need your:

  • study name
  • study ID
  • email address

We’ll review your pre-screener and contact you when we’ve approved it or if there are any changes needed before it can go live.
