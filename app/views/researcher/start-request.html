Create a study request

This guided process helps you check feasibility and submit a study for recruitment on the platform. You can save and return at any time.

First, you’ll check whether there are enough potential volunteers (feasibility). Then you’ll provide your study details for admin review and publishing.

What you’ll do

  1. Check feasibility: demographics, medical criteria, and sites.
  2. Identify the study with CPMS/IRAS or enter details manually.
  3. Provide study information and study type.
  4. Confirm requirements (demographics, medical, sites) and target recruitment date.
  5. Tell us about ethics approval. You can upload proof now or add it later.

What you’ll need

  • Study title and lay summary (or CPMS/IRAS IDs)
  • Eligibility criteria (age, sex, relevant regions) and medical conditions of interest
  • Participating site codes (ODS/Trust/organisation codes if known)
  • Target recruitment date
  • Ethics approval reference/date or approval letter (optional for now)
