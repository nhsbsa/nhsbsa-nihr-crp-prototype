Register with Be Part of Research

In order to register for Be Part of Research you need to be:

  • over 18

  • have a UK address

  • have a valid UK phone number

  • have a valid email

Alternatively, if you live in England or Wales, you can use your NHS login details to register. If you use NHS login, you will need to use this option each time you access your account or update your details. Once you have registered, we will use your information to send you relevant research opportunities.
